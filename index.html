About Us

Welcome to TAS Hydraulics, your trusted destination for high-quality Hydraulic Gear Pumps. As a premier provider, we collaborate with leading manufacturers to offer a comprehensive range of hydraulic gear pumps, catering to diverse industrial needs. Our product portfolio spans from 0.08 cc/rev to 66.67 cc/rev, providing a fluid flow capacity ranging from 1.2 LPM to 100 LPM.With over two decades of expertise,our journey began as a breakdown service under the ENZED brand, evolving into a comprehensive end-to-end service provider for Tasmania's manufacturing and primary industries.
